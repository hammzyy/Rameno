HUNTER X HUNTER

DESCRIPTION/REVIEW

                The story focuses on a youngboy named
                Gon Freecss who discovers that his father,
                who left him at a young age,is actually a 
                world-renowed Hunter,a licensed professional
                who specializes in fantastical pursuits such 
                as locating rare or unidentified animal species,
                treasure hunting,surveying unexplored enclaves,
                or hunting down lawless individuals.Gon departs
                on a journey to become a hunter and eventually 
                find his father. Along the way, Gon meets various
                other Hunters and encounters the paranormal.
